Key Benefits

What HB2089 Does for Veterans

This bill provides meaningful tax relief to the veterans who sacrificed for our state and nation.

Homestead Exemption

Exempt up to $250,000 of home value from property tax for eligible disabled veterans.

Property Tax Relief

Includes personal property exemptions to reduce the overall tax burden on veteran households.

Housing Cost Alignment

Proposed increase aligns Missouri with rising housing costs and neighboring state standards.

Keep Vets in Their Homes

Prevents disabled veterans from being priced out of homes they’ve lived in for years.

State Parity

Brings Missouri in line with neighboring states that already offer similar veteran protections.

Bipartisan Support

Supporting veterans transcends party lines. This bill benefits all Missouri communities.
