Welcome to MoVets.org

A non-partisan, grassroots effort to help Missouri's disabled veterans get fair property tax relief.

MoVets.org launched as a simple idea: make it effortless for any Missourian to find their state representative and voice their support for HB2089 — the Disabled Veterans Homestead Exemption.

Missouri is home to more than 341,000 veterans. Tens of thousands live with service-connected disabilities while facing rising property tax bills on homes they've owned for decades. HB2089 provides meaningful relief — tiered by disability rating — so the veterans who served don't get priced out of the homes they've earned.

What we do

  • Explain the bill in plain language — no legalese.
  • Help you find your rep with an interactive district map.
  • Make contact easy — pre-filled messages you can send in under two minutes.
  • Track impact publicly on our Our Impact page.

What we don't do

No fundraising. No party politics. No data selling. We built this with our own time because Missouri's veterans deserve a clear path to the relief they've earned.
